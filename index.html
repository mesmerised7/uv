Bham Forging Works - Since 1964

Founded in 1964, our company in Ludhiana is a premier gear manufacturing specializing in the production of high-quality gear components, precision gears, industrial gears, custom gears, gear shafts, and axles for a wide range of applications. With over six decades of experience, we are recognized as a trusted gear manufacturing company committed to delivering superior geared solutions and machinery parts. Our factory, strategically located on Gill Road near Fashion King, integrates the latest gear manufacturing technology with skilled craftsmanship, ensuring top-tier products that meet international standards.

As a leading gear parts manufacturer in Ludhiana, we emphasize precision engineering, offering gear cutting, gear grinding, and gear assembly services. Our expert team leverages advanced tools to create high-performance gears that cater to diverse industries, including agricultural machinery, automotive, and industrial sectors. Our commitment to gearing solutions and quality has solidified our position as one of the most reliable gear manufacturing in the region. We focus on providing customized gear solutions tailored to the specific needs of our customers. From bevel gears to spur gears, our comprehensive product range ensures that we meet the demands of modern industries while maintaining superior gear durability and efficiency. As we continue to expand globally, we remain dedicated to producing precision-engineered gears and maintaining our legacy of reliable gear manufacturing.

As we embark on our journey toward global expansion, we remain deeply grateful for the trust bestowed upon us by our valued customers. This trust forms the foundation of our unwavering commitment to gear manufacturing in Ludhiana, where we specialize in producing industrial gears, spur gears, helical gears, bevel gears, and worm gears with unmatched precision and durability. With a focus on cutting-edge technology and advanced manufacturing processes, we continue to deliver high-performance transmission solutions for industries such as agriculture, automotive, oil mills, sugar mills, and heavy machinery. As we expand into new markets and forge lasting partnerships, we remain dedicated to enhancing our production capabilities and setting new benchmarks in Ludhiana’s gear industry. With gratitude in our hearts and a steadfast resolve, we pledge to continue our legacy of reliable, high-quality gear manufacturing, serving clients across the globe with superior products and customer-centric services.
