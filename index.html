High Precisioned Components for Automotive and Industrial Applications

Forging industries since 1964

Established in 1964, our Ludhiana-based business stands as a stalwart in the realm of agricultural machinery components. Boasting an illustrious legacy spanning six decades, we have honed our expertise in crafting precision gears, shafts, axles, and an array of other vital components. Nestled on Gill Road, adjacent to Fashion King, our factory epitomizes a fusion of dedication and innovation, serving as a beacon of excellence in the industry.

Our journey is steeped in a rich tradition of precision engineering, unwavering commitment to quality, and an unyielding dedication to customer satisfaction. Each product that leaves our facility bears the hallmark of our meticulous craftsmanship and adherence to the highest standards of excellence. At the heart of our success lies a relentless pursuit of advancement through cutting-edge technology and a highly skilled workforce, ensuring that our offerings surpass expectations at every turn.

Backed by a robust distribution network and unwavering customer support, we prioritize prompt delivery and exceptional service, cementing our reputation as a trusted partner in the agricultural machinery sector. Our unwavering commitment to innovation propels us to anticipate market needs and remain at the forefront of the industry through continuous research and strategic partnerships.

As we embark on our journey toward global expansion, we remain deeply grateful for the trust bestowed upon us by our valued customers. It is this trust that serves as the cornerstone of our commitment to upholding our standards of excellence as we venture into new territories and forge new relationships across the globe. With gratitude in our hearts and a steadfast resolve, we pledge to continue our legacy of precision, quality, and customer-centricity for generations to come.
